Types of Assistive Devices

  • Mobility Aids: Devices that help people move around, such as wheelchairs, crutches, or walkers.
  • Hearing Aids: Electronic devices that amplify sound for people with hearing loss.
  • Visual Aids: Tools that help people with vision impairments, like magnifiers or screen readers.
  • Communication Tools: Devices for individuals with speech or language difficulties, such as text-to-speech software.
